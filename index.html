About Me

Hello, my name is Giselle! I was born and raised in New York City. I am half Colombian and speak fluent Spanish. I hold a B.A. in Mass Communication with a minor in Interactive Media from the University of Delaware. I currently work on the digital team at an advertising agency, and have previously worked freelance as a front-end web developer. I have also worked as a graphic designer in London for Tibet Foundation, a registered non-political charity working for and with the Tibetan people, both inside Tibet and across the Tibetan diaspora.

I have lived, and worked, on three continents. My hobbies and interests include cycling, hiking, traveling, reading and listening to live music. I ride a Specialized Dolce Sport and sincerely look forward to never owning a car. My favorite food is cherimoya, also known as custard apple.
